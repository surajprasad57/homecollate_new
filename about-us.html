About Us

HomeCollate is a user-friendly digital platform, which brings a new experience of comparing different Accomodation companies. OUR MISSION is "To provide the easiest way to compare different Accomodation Companies". Our 24/7 support team of experts are committed to helping you discover, compare, and book value-for-money student accommodation at no additional cost.

Our Story

Our journey started with a simple discussion on the challenges faced by the students, in finding a suitable accomodation in an unknown city. So with the aim of solving this common issue, we came up with an idea of comparing different PG companies under the same roof. All our team members work day and night to implement this idea into reality. This leads to the succesful launching of HomeCollate in October, 2018.
